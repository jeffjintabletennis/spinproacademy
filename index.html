About Us

SpinPro Academy is led by Coach Jeff Jin, who is a Queensland state champion and Queensland representative at the Australian National Championships. Before moving to Australia, Jeff served as a table tennis teacher for a decade in Anhui, China, developing strong technical foundations for juniors and adults alike.
